Search and Filter

Typing a text in the search bar will result in only the travels, that have that text in their name, to be shown. This is helpful for finding a specific travel arrangement.

Clicking on the Filter burron opens the Filter dialog box. In it, you can set the minimum and maximum values of the travels you want to be shown. Click Apply to apply the filters for to your search.

Clicking on the < and > buttons (if those are present) changes the month for displayed travels.

Listed Travels

Selecting one of the listed travels on your left will display the details in the bottom of the screen, list the various locations in that travel in the bottom right and show the travel route on the map.

After selecting a listed travel, you will be able to click the Book this travel button in the bottom of the screen and go straight to Reserved Trips.

Map

The map displays the locations (blue pins) and the route (orange lines) of the selected travel.
